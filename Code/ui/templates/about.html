How It Works

The process is simple: first, you will upload a PDF version of your patent draft to the PatentNerd tool. PatentNerd will then use LLMs to evaluate whether your patent's claims are fully supported by its body, a common sticking point in court cases. PatentNerd focuses on clarity and claim support, and in addition to flagging ambiguous sections of your patent, will suggest terms that remain consistent with the patent body, making claims more robust. PatentNerd will both find problems and suggest solutions!

With this tool, we aim to make it easier for you to understand potential issues in your patent draft to fix before submitting your final draft.

*PatentNerd is a research tool and is not intended to substitute for professional legal advice. We recommend seeking professional legal advice before submitting a patent.
